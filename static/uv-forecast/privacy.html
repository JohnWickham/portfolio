UV Forecast

Privacy Policy

The UV Forecast app is designed to collect as little information about you as possible.

When you grant the app permission to use your current location, your current latitude and longitude coordinates are sent to the app’s data provider, Dark Sky, to load weather data for your location. The request containing location information can not be used to identify you or your device individually, and isn’t sent to any other company or service.

The app doesn’t include any third-party code, including analytics and tracking frameworks.
