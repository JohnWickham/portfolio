Prototask

2017 iOS DESIGN + DEVELOPMENT

Prototask is a list-keeping app for iOS that uses Core Data for persistence. The interface is gesture-driven—making it remarkably clear and beautiful—and made entirely with UIKit and Auto Layout. The app takes advantage of familiar system features like shake-to-undo, 3D Touch, and haptic feedback to make it feel right at home to users of iOS.

Pitchfork

2017 iOS DESIGN + DEVELOPMENT

A tool that changes the speed and pitch of songs in your music library (now with nearly 40,000 users worldwide). It’s made with AVFoundation, MusicKit, and Auto Layout.

Featured by Apple on the App Store
